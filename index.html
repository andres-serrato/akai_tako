About us

Japanese cuisine is an art in which aesthetics stem from product freshness and quality and where preparation is as critical as presentation. The dishes must be pleasing both to the eye and palate, making Japanese cuisine a truly sensual experience.

Akai Taco is serving authentic Japanese cuisine to everyone in Montreal with its large collection of all famous Japanese dishes. Our chefs closely observe the freshness of ingredients on daily basis to ensure the same great taste filled with rich flavours every time. Come and try our appetizing Japanese food at our newly decorated restaurant space.
